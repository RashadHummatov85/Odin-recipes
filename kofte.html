Kofte

Description

Kofte is a popular Turkish meatball dish, made with ground beef or lamb and seasoned with a variety of spices. It can be grilled, baked, or pan-fried.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into small meatballs or patties.
  3. Cook on a grill, bake in the oven, or pan-fry until browned and fully cooked.
  4. Serve hot with rice or salad.
