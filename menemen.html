Menemen

Description

Menemen is a traditional Turkish dish made with eggs, tomatoes, peppers, and spices. It is often served for breakfast and is quick and easy to make. The dish is both flavorful and healthy, making it a favorite among locals and tourists alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, parsley

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped green pepper and saut√© for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until softened.
  4. Crack the eggs into the pan and stir gently until scrambled.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and optional red pepper flakes.
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
