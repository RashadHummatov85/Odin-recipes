Baklava

Description

Baklava is a rich, sweet pastry made with layers of filo dough, chopped nuts, and sweet syrup. It's a popular dessert in Turkey and other Middle Eastern countries.

Ingredients

  • 500g filo dough
  • 300g walnuts or pistachios, chopped
  • 200g butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Layer the filo dough sheets in a baking dish, brushing each sheet with melted butter.
  3. Sprinkle the chopped nuts between every few layers of filo dough.
  4. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Prepare a syrup by boiling sugar, water, honey, and vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the syrup over the baked baklava and allow it to soak in before serving.
